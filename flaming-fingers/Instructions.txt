How to run TypeRacer
1.	Open JGrasp
2.	Find the "Final Project" folder
3.	Compile and run the Driver.java (NOT GameDriver)
4.	Enjoy!

How to play TypeRacer
1.	Pick level 1, 2 or 3
   a. Level 1 features easier paragraphs, with shorter words, simpler sentence structure and punctuation, 
      and easier vocabulary
   b.	Level 2 displays slightly harder passages, taken from major authors, textbooks, and works of literature. 
      There is significantly harder vocabulary and sentence structure
   c.	Level 3 returns the hardest passages of all that will really trip your typing fingers up! Watch out for 
      paragraphs in latin, paragraphs with weird vocabulary and diverse punctuation, and paragraphs from 
      high-level literature and textbooks. 
   d. Use the Test Level mode to try out all the features with a short and simple sentence. Perfect for grading ;) 
      
2.	Enter a number of players

3.	Enter player names (optional)

4.	Click begin! (takes you to Gameboard)

5.	Click start and get typing!

6.	If you’re in 2 player mode, after player 1 finishes, the second player will get a reset frame 
   with the same paragraph
   
7.	If you need to go or need to take a break, leave the game by pressing the home button, which will take 
   you to the title screen
   
8.	When done, click the scores button to view your statistics from the round!

                                                     HAVE FUN!
